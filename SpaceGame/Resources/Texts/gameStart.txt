Welcome to Stellar Prospects demo!

This game is controlled entirely through the console that you have in front of you. You will type commands and pass arguments to the commands to control the game. The objective of this game is to control the ships at your disposal and mine minerals from an asteroid field. You will have 1 year to collect as many minerals as possible. To check on other commands at any point in the game, use the help command. Start by typing 'help' and hit enter.
